About Me

I’m Shanice Ndungaua — a multi-talented creative based in Namibia. I studied Graphic Design, Photography, Web Design, and Software Development at the College Of The Arts in Windhoek. I love creating visuals that tell stories, express emotion, and connect with people.

My creative style is a blend of modern minimalism and vibrant storytelling. I enjoy experimenting with colors, textures, and concepts that reflect individuality and culture.
